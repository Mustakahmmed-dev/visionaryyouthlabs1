About the Founder

Mustak Ahmmed

Mustak Ahmmed is a Rohingya Tech Enthusiast working in the Digital Marketing industry for the last 4 years and coming with a strong Marketing Strategies along with pursuing his education in the Computer Programming with the aim of Software Engineering.

Learning something is not something only you learn for yourself but to serve for the society, community and the whole world to create a better and peaceful environment. Most of the crimes come from those who are unemployed and this is where I want to create a space where anyone can learn and get employed, that's my vision inside the most persecuted community known as Rohingya muslims.

I'm a Rohingya, born in Arakan State of Myanmar and had to flee the country to save our life at the age of 11 years. Many people were killed and many were sent to jail and most were broken by beating and I found in my mind that it's something general that has been in our eye since we were born. But at that time, I didn't know that we the Rohingya ethnic has been facing such atrocities and genocides for decades, more than hundreds of years and since we had fled the country and started to educate ourselves, I started to understand the nature, the world and what happened and happening on us. I found as a result more than 5 lacs of Rohingya muslims were killed inside the Myanmar ground. Learn More About Rohingya Muslims

After all, I found myself as a Tech Enthusiast and learn more about technology then create job opportunities, skill development opportunities for the marginalized Rohingya community.
Now, we are working with a number of 10 Team members where we help many businesses with Marketing Solutions, IT services and Solutions across the world. Visit our services
